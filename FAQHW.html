Frequently Asked Questions

  1. What is the HungryWungry app all about?

    HungryWungry is the world's first digital platform made exclusively for today's college-going students. It connects them with popular eateries and shopping destinations where they can avail whacky deals, deadly discounts, and amazing offers across multiple segments like Food & Beverage, Fashion, Retail, and Electronics to name a few.

  2. How do I access the offers and deals listed on HungryWungry?

    In order to access the offers listed on the HungryWungry app, you must be at least 17 years of age, a student in one of the colleges listed with HungryWungry, and possess a valid college ID card.

  3. Where do I access HungryWungry from?

    HungryWungry app is available for download on both Android as well as iOS platforms.

  4. Are there any charges to download the HungryWungry app?

    No. The HungryWungry app is absolutely free to download.

  5. How do I register with HungryWungry?

    Download the HungryWungry app and complete the one-time registration process. You will be asked to verify your mobile number and fill in basic details like your name, email address, date of birth along with state and city. You will also need to select your college from the list of colleges and upload a soft copy of your college ID card. The details submitted will be sent for verification.

  6. How do I know if my application is accepted or rejected?

    In either case, you will be shared the status of your application on the email address provided. Additionally, the next time you try and login, you can also see the status there. If your application is accepted, you can log into the app and start using the services.

  7. What if there are errors in the form submitted for verification?

    The details submitted in the application will be thoroughly verified. The ones where there is an issue either with mobile number or email address or the ID card is not readable, such applications are rejected.

  8. What if my college is not listed with HungryWungry?

    If your college is not already listed, you can share the details of your college while filling up the registration form. The college will be shown in the list of colleges once our verification process is completed.

  9. What if my ID card is lost or damaged?

    Unfortunately, there is no other way to verify that you are genuinely a student and studying in a particular college. If you are unable to furnish a soft copy of the ID card or your ID card is damaged to an extent where it is not possible to read the contents clearly, you may not be allowed to complete the registration process or your application may end up being rejected.

  10. What happens if my college listing request is rejected?

    Students from a particular college will not be able to create an account with HungryWungry until the college's information is successfully verified by us, and the college is visible in the college list. In case of a college listing being rejected, we suggest re-initializing the listing process.
