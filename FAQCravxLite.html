Frequently Asked Questions

  1. What is the Cravx Lite app?

    Cravx Lite is a dedicated tool for our business partners to help them make smart business decisions and effectively manage their digital promotions. With Cravx Lite, partners can create exciting deals & discounts and also exclusive limited-time offers to attract the target customer base.

  2. How do I register for Cravx Lite?

    Kindly visit www.hungrywungry.com and leave your contact details. Our representative will get in touch to guide you through the process and ensure smooth on-boarding.

  3. What all can I do with the Cravx Lite app?

    With the Cravx Lite application, a business can easily create attractive offers and deals exclusively for college-going students to boost its revenues. Once the offers are added, it can keep track of how it is faring. To ensure data privacy and safety, we encourage partners to add sub-users who would have access to only redemption part thereby not seeing any sensitive data.

  4. Can multiple people use the same app?

    We strongly recommend that you create sub-users for each of your customer-facing staff. This will ensure that there is no dependency on an individual at the same time the user will have access to the data which the sub-users cannot see.

  5. What are ‘Cards’ in the app?

    Cards are nothing but offers created for a specific segment of users. These cards may be for a closed group of users or open to all. As a business, you may add your own offer to any of the cards visible to enhance your business opportunities.

  6. How does a user redeem an offer?

    The process is quick, secure, and simple. Each offer comes with a unique QR code assigned to it. Whenever a user wants to redeem an offer, he/she will show the unique QR code in his app. The Cravx Lite app comes with a built-in QR code scanner which can read the contents and tell you which offer to provide.

  7. What if I wish to change an offer mid-way?

    Though we suggest our partners not change offers mid-way to ensure stability, if you still wish to do so, you may connect with your relationship manager or write to us at support@cravx.com.

  8. How do I access Cravx Lite?

    The Cravx Lite app can be downloaded from the Google Play Store or the Apple App Store. Cravx Lite can also be accessed through the web.

  9. Is there a restriction to how many staff members can I add?

    No. There is no restriction as such.

  10. Can I refuse to give an offer to a member?

    The partner reserves the right to decline the request for an offer if the user is not carrying the registered user app.
